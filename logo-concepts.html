Logo Concepts

Six directions for The Dandelion Effect. Each one tells the story differently.

Concept 1 — The Single Seed

Minimal and elegant. One seed, mid-flight. Says everything without trying too hard. Works beautifully at any size.

Concept 2 — Seeds in Motion

The seeds scattering shows the ripple effect — change spreading from one woman to everyone around her. Movement and energy.

Concept 3 — The Monogram

Bold "DE" lettermark. Modern, memorable, works as a favicon or app icon. The seeds keep it connected to the story.

Concept 4 — The Clock

A full dandelion clock — the moment before the seeds scatter. Beautiful, botanical, timeless. One seed is already breaking free.

Concept 5 — The Wordmark

The seed replaces the dot on the 'i' in Dandelion. Subtle, clever, built into the name itself. Clean and professional.

Concept 6 — Root & Rise

A dandelion growing through concrete. Roots visible below, seeds releasing above. The full story of resilience in one mark. "Rooted. Resilient. Rising."
