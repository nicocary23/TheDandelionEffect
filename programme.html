What's Included — Week by Week

1

Your Starting Point

One-to-one video call. Understanding where you are, what you need, what's failed in the past, your struggles, your pinch points, your goals. A real conversation. Building connection first and understanding what you need.

2-3

Building The Foundation

3 x 60 min weight-based training sessions per week (main lift, accessories, heart raiser). Plus a 4th optional movement day. Food diary review to build mindfulness around eating.

4

The Mindset Shift Begins

Weekly WhatsApp check-in (message, voice note, or call), spending time talking about building resilient habits. This is where physical and mental strength start to merge.

5-6

Finding Your Rhythm

Training consistency builds. Monthly group movement session (walk, bond, community). Optional bi-monthly live session on resilience, difficult conversations, and what actually affects women our age.

✓

Progress Review

Online video call reviewing your progress: strength, weight, and most importantly — how your life feels different.

7-8

Becoming Her

You start to feel it. Stronger. Clearer. More yourself than you've been in years. The promises you make to yourself start sticking. The woman looking back at you in the mirror is someone you recognise again — and someone you're proud of. There isn't a better feeling than knowing you made positive changes because you worked your ass off to get there.
