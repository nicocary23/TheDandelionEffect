Seeds in Motion

Six variations on your favourite concept. Same idea — seeds scattering, change spreading — explored in different moods and layouts.

2A — Upward Drift

Seeds rising up from the text like thoughts taking flight. Gentle, aspirational. The further they go, the more they fade — spreading quietly.

2B — Windswept

A trail of seeds carried by wind from a single source. The whole name on one line underneath. Clean, directional energy. One thing leading to another.

2C — Constellation

Seeds scattered like stars across a dark sky with fine connecting lines. The change has already happened — it's everywhere. Powerful on dark backgrounds.

2D — The Spiral

Seeds spiralling outward from a single source. Organic, natural movement. Feels like watching it happen in slow motion. Tagline: "Change that spreads."

2E — Bold & Warm

Tighter cluster of seeds with bolder typography. Confident. No tagline, no explanation — just the name and the energy. Strong on the deep green.

2F — The Full Banner

Wide format for website headers and social banners. Seeds on both sides framing the name. "Effect" in terracotta. Tagline below: "Mindset. Strength. Community." Clean and premium.
